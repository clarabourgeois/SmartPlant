Our Roots

Taking care of a plant is not limited to watering it regularly. Each plant species has specific needs in terms of humidity, temperature, and light exposure. These parameters can vary depending on the conditions of the room where the plant is placed.

The SmartPlant project offers a simple solution to monitor these essential parameters for the health of your plants. With the help of sensors placed directly in the pot, it becomes possible to measure the room temperature and soil moisture level in real time. This data is then compared to the optimal values for the specific plant.

The system provides practical recommendations such as:

  • Checking if the room temperature is suitable or if the plant needs to be moved.
  • Monitoring whether the humidity level is sufficient or if watering is necessary.
  • Confirming that the current conditions are ideal for the plant.

The goal of SmartPlant is to assist plant enthusiasts, whether beginners or experienced, by providing accurate information to ensure an optimal environment for their indoor plants.
